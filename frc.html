What is FIRST Robotics Competition?

FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition) is one of the world's most prestigious robotics competitions for high school students, organized to develop engineering, software, design, and teamwork skills.

The competition focuses not only on technical knowledge but also on innovation, problem-solving, time management, and team coordination. Participants design, build, and program robots under real engineering conditions to tackle challenges.

However, FRC is not just a robotics competition; with the motto "More than Robots," it emphasizes values such as community contribution, leadership, and inspiration. Teams support local communities through various social responsibility projects.

The concepts of "Gracious Professionalism" and "Coopertition" (collaboration while competing) form the spirit of FRC, making it not just a technical platform but also a social and ethical learning environment.
